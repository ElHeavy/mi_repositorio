ABOUT EL HEAVY

Where it all began...

El Heavy traded his Sega for a guitar when he turned 15. Dissatisfied with the local instructors, El Heavy began teaching himself through the MEGADETH - Rust in Peace sheet music book and various other instructional books and videos. During high school, El Heavy took music theory classes.
